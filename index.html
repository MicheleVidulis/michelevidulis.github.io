I am a PhD student at the Geometric Computing Laboratory, EPFL, advised by Prof. Mark Pauly. I am interested in physics-based simulation, optimization, computational design, and digital fabrication.

During my PhD, I was fortunate to work with Prof. Julian Panetta (UC Davis) and Prof. Eitan Grinspun (University of Toronto). I interned at Disney Research | Studios working in the Rendering and Simulation group with Vinicius Da Costa De Azevedo and Jingwei Tang.

I hold a double MSc in Computational Science and Engineering from EPFL and Politecnico di Milano. I received my BSc in Mathematical Engineering at Politecnico di Milano.
